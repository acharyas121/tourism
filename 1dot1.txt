Short Info: Pashupatinath Temple is a religious site for Hindu followers. Lord Shiva is worshipped in Pashupatinath. Only Hindus are allowed to enter inside the main temple. It is highly crowded with the devotees during festivals like MahaShivaratri where devotees from other countries like India come here to worship.
Audience: It is suitable for viewing of Hindu traditions of worships and faith. It is an example of Nepali Hindu culture and traditions. You will get to watch cremation of dead bodies in live and the 'Aarti' at evenings. 
Age Range: Toursists of all age can visit this temple.
